07 · What SARVAX executes

Do not build a third note-taker.

The opening is an execution and governance layer for wealth firms—especially in India—where approved work crosses fragmented systems and regulation matters more than a generic chat interface.

1

Lead with approved workflows

Show the trigger, systems touched, approval owner, evidence captured, retry policy and final outcome. Make execution visible.

2

Own the India connector map

Prioritize WhatsApp Business, Zoho, Indian wealth infrastructure, document rails and compliance requirements that US rivals do not cover deeply.

3

Make proof part of execution

Every completed workflow should carry the trigger, approvals, systems touched, evidence captured, exceptions and final outcome. This is the trust surface buyers can audit.

4

Turn meetings into triggers

Meeting intelligence remains native, but its job is to start governed work: suitability checks, onboarding, account servicing, QA, compliance and follow-up.

5

Measure completed outcomes

Report cycle time, manual touches removed, open-loop rate, exceptions, revenue opportunities completed and compliance evidence—not just hours saved.

6

Package a focused land-and-expand offer

Sell one high-impact workflow to a firm, integrate with its real stack, prove outcome quality, then expand by workflow and team.

Critical watchlist for the next 180 days

Jump: Onboard adoption, AI Associate action depth and enterprise contracts. Zocks: Client Queries conversion, MCP ecosystem expansion and completion of “coming soon” capabilities. For both: any disclosed ARR, retention, paid-active definitions or defensible valuation.
